A simple avocado & toast sandwich

A simple sunny side egg on avocado buttered toast.

Stuff that you need

  • An egg
  • A ripe avocado
  • Some unsalted butter
  • A slice of bread (can be any brand)

How to cook it

  1. Heat up a wok/pan
  2. Place a small silver of butter onto the hot pan and coat it
  3. Butter up both sides of a slice of bread
  4. Place the bread on the pan and lightly toast both sides, till it is a little light brown
  5. Take the bread out and place it on a plate
  6. Take a few ripe avocados and mash them
  7. Spread the mashed avocado across the bread
  8. Crack an egg and cook it on a hot pan
  9. After cooking the egg for a while (add salt/pepper to taste), wait till it's egg white is white, then take it off the pan
  10. Place the sunny side egg on your avocado toast and enjoy
