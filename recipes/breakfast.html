A simple avocado & toast sandwich

Description

A simple sunny side egg on avocado buttered toast.

How to cook it

  1. Heat up a wok/pan
  2. Place a small silver of butter onto the hot pan and coat it
  3. Butter up both sides of a slice of bread
  4. Place the bread on the pan and lightly toast both sides, till it is a little light brown
  5. Take the bread out and place it on a plate
  6. Take a few ripe avocados and mash them
