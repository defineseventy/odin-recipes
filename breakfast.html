A simple egg & toast sandwich

Description

A simple sunny side egg on avacado buttered toast.

How to cook it

  • Heat up a wok/pan
  • Place a small silver of butter onto the hot pan and coat it
  • Butter up both sides of a slice of bread
  • Place the bread on the pan and lightly toast both sides, till it is a little light brown
  • Take the bread out and place it on a plate
